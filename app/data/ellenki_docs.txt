Ellenki College of Engineering and Technology (ECET) is an autonomous engineering college located in Patelguda village, Ameenpur mandal, in the Sangareddy district of the Hyderabad region of Telangana. The campus is situated near BHEL and offers a calm learning environment while still being connected to the city. The college is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad and its programmes are approved by AICTE. It has been serving students for more than two decades and focuses on providing quality technical education to learners from both urban and rural backgrounds.

The college was established under the leadership of Dr. E. Sadasiva Reddy, who has experience in running several educational institutions in Telangana. The institution aims to give equal opportunities to students from small towns and villages so they can pursue their engineering and management aspirations. Ellenki is recognised as an autonomous institute, which allows it to design industry-aligned curricula and update its academic regulations more flexibly while retaining its affiliation to JNTUH.

The vision of Ellenki College is to be recognised as a leading institution in engineering and technology education, known for nurturing innovative thinkers and graduates who create a positive impact on society. Its mission is to offer a rigorous and well-rounded education that emphasises discipline, quality and creativity. With the help of dedicated faculty and modern facilities, the college works to inspire students to excel in their chosen fields and contribute responsibly to the community.

Academically, Ellenki offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma programmes. At the B.Tech level, the college provides programmes in Computer Science and Engineering, CSE with Data Science, CSE with Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, CSE with Cyber Security, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Civil Engineering. At the postgraduate level, the college offers M.Tech degrees in CAD/CAM, Computer Science and Engineering, Embedded Systems, Power Electronics and Structural Engineering. In addition, the college offers MBA and MCA programmes as well as diploma programmes in Civil, Computer, Electronics and Communication, Electrical and Electronics and Mechanical engineering disciplines.

The admissions process follows state-level regulations. Most B.Tech seats are filled through the state entrance examination and counselling process, while a portion of seats is available under the management quota. For management quota admissions in B.Tech, students are generally required to either hold a valid rank or secure a minimum aggregate percentage in the core subjects of Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry at the intermediate level. A specified percentage of total seats is reserved for the management quota and candidates must satisfy the eligibility conditions mentioned by the college. Admissions to MBA and MCA programmes usually consider TS ICET rankings, while M.Tech admissions are based on TS PGECET or equivalent examinations. The college’s counselling code for engineering admissions is ELEN, and prospective students can contact the admissions office through the email address and phone numbers given on the website.

The infrastructure at Ellenki College includes classrooms, department-specific laboratories, computer centres and seminar halls designed to support engineering and management education. Laboratories are set up for CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical and Civil branches so that students can perform curriculum-based experiments and project work. The campus also has a central library with reference books, textbooks, journals and digital resources that support academic and research needs. Additional facilities include a cafeteria, common areas for students, basic medical support and spaces where events and academic activities can be conducted.

To support students who live far from the campus, the college provides hostel facilities. The hostels are intended to offer a safe and disciplined environment with essential amenities for residential students. For day scholars, Ellenki operates bus services that connect the campus with various parts of Hyderabad and nearby localities. The focus is on providing a reliable and reasonably comfortable transportation option so that students, staff and faculty can commute without difficulty.

The Training and Placement Cell at Ellenki plays an important role in preparing students for the job market. The cell organises training in aptitude, communication skills, group discussions and interviews. It also coordinates with companies to conduct campus recruitment drives. Over the years, the college has built relationships with organisations from IT, core engineering and other sectors, and many of these firms visit the campus to recruit students. The placement cell works to align training activities with industry expectations so that graduating students become more employable.

Campus life at Ellenki includes technical, cultural and sports activities. Students can participate in technical clubs, coding and project events, workshops, seminars and guest lectures that help them apply theoretical knowledge to practical problems. Cultural events, sports competitions and fests are conducted to encourage teamwork, creativity and leadership. The college also promotes extracurricular and social welfare activities, including student clubs related to artificial intelligence, robotics, web development, entrepreneurship and other interest-based groups. Through these activities, students are encouraged to develop both technical and soft skills alongside their regular academics.
